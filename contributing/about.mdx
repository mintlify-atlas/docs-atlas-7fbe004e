## Platohedro and Matinée

**Platohedro** is a creative and educational space located in Medellín, Colombia, that brings together pedagogy, digital fabrication, and artistic expression. One of its ongoing programs is **Matinée** — a workshop series designed for children where play, imagination, and collaborative learning come together in the same room.

More than **100 children** from the neighbourhood have taken part in Matinée sessions, building and exploring through hands-on activities that blend physical and digital making.

---

## How this project started

*Yo Quiero Aprender* grew out of the Matinée program as a digital prototype. In 2025, the project received a grant from the **Presupuesto Participativo de la Comuna 9 – Buenos Aires** of Medellín, under the *Narrativas Digitales y Expresión Cultural en Arte, Cultura, Ciencia y Tecnología* funding line.

That support made it possible to turn the sessions' outputs — songs, characters, reflections on self-care, and experiments — into the interactive experiences you can explore in this app. The children's voices, questions, and ideas are the real content; the code is just the container.

---

## Vision

Quiero Aprender is designed as an **open prototype** — not a finished product. Any community group, school, or cultural organisation can fork the repository, swap out the content, and adapt the experiences to their own participants and context.

## What comes next

The project is actively evolving. Areas the Platohedro team is exploring for future iterations:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Camera integration">
    Allowing participants to photograph their own drawings and artworks to use as the content inside experiences like the character gallery, replacing the existing placeholder models with real images made by children.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Saving favourite combinations">
    Letting players bookmark their favourite slot-machine creatures, watercolour paintings, or sound seeds so they can revisit and share them in a later session.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Facilitator guides">
    Structured pedagogical notes for teachers and workshop leaders — one per chapter — explaining how to frame each experience, suggested discussion prompts, and ways to extend the activity offline.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
