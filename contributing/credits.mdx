## Team

**Design and facilitation** — Equipo Yo Quiero Aprender · Conexión Platohedro

The experiences in this app were shaped by the children and facilitators of the Matinée program at Platohedro in Medellín. The ideas, characters, sounds, and self-care reflections that power each chapter come directly from their sessions.

**Prototyping and development** — Built with open-source technologies and collaborative care.

## Open-source stack

Quiero Aprender is built entirely on free and open-source tools:

| Technology | Version | Role |
|---|---|---|
| [Next.js](https://nextjs.org) | 14.2.5 | App framework, routing, static optimisation |
| [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org) | 5.4.5 | Type safety across all components |
| [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com) | 3.4.4 | Utility-first styling |
| [React Three Fiber](https://docs.pmnd.rs/react-three-fiber) | 8.15.16 | 3D scenes and character gallery |
| [@react-three/drei](https://github.com/pmndrs/drei) | 9.97.0 | Helpers for R3F (OrbitControls, loaders, etc.) |
| [Three.js](https://threejs.org) | 0.160.0 | Underlying 3D engine |
| Web Audio API | — | Sound synthesis for the seed and marimba experiences |
